About Rentoc Reflux

Rentoc Reflux is a dynamic startup dedicated to revolutionizing the steel industry through the supply of premium materials. Founded in 2022, we have quickly established ourselves as a trusted partner for steel manufacturers worldwide. Our core focus lies in providing advanced solutions based on fluorides, carbon, and aluminum, ensuring unparalleled quality, efficiency, and innovation in every product we deliver.

At Rentoc Reflux, we believe in pushing the boundaries of what's possible in material science. Our commitment to research and development allows us to stay ahead of industry trends, offering materials that enhance performance, reduce costs, and promote sustainability in steel production.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to empower the steel industry with cutting-edge materials that drive efficiency and innovation. We envision a future where sustainable practices and advanced technologies converge to create stronger, more resilient steel products for global infrastructure and manufacturing.

Guided by principles of integrity, excellence, and customer-centricity, we strive to build long-lasting partnerships that foster growth and success for all stakeholders.

Our Products

We specialize in high-quality materials tailored for the steel industry:

Fluorides

Advanced fluoride-based compounds that improve fluxing and refining processes in steel production, enhancing purity and reducing impurities.

Carbon

Premium carbon additives that optimize carbon content, improving strength and durability while maintaining precise control over alloy compositions.

Aluminum

High-grade aluminum solutions for deoxidation and alloying, ensuring superior metallurgical properties and efficiency in steel manufacturing.
